Once upon a time, there were two little sisters, Red Riding Hood and Yellow Riding Hood, who lived in a forest. On the other side of that forest lived their grandma, whose affection the two sisters rabidly competed for. On grandma’s birthday, whoever got to her house with their baskets full of gifts would definitely win grandma’s affection forever after. But we all know how fierce siblings’ competition can be…

You can choose to be either Red Riding Hood or Yellow Riding Hood.

To win: you must be the first one to reach grandma’s house OR tap into your inner wolf and devour your sister!
